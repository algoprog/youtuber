- How to download a video from a website?

1. Enter the url of the video you want to download.
2. Click the "Download" button.
3. Choose the location to save the video.

 - What's the "Download url created"?

The Download url created is a shortened url of the video download link. You can share this url with your friends to let them download the video instantly.

 - What's the qr-code and how to use it?

The qr-code is an image created that contains the download url of the video you want to download. Qr-codes can be read by your mobile phone with qr-readers apps.

 - How to convert a video using YouTuber?

1. Choose the video you want to convert
2. Choose the video type you want to be converted
3. Click Convert and wait until video conversion finishes.
4. The converted video is in the same folder with the input video.
5. If you want to play the converted video just click Play Converted.

 - What video types can YouTuber convert?

YouTuber can convert:

- Windows Media Videos (V.7 WMV)
- Xvid MPEG-4 Codec (AVI)
- iPod Videos (Apple QuickTime MOV)
- iPhone Videos (MPEG-4 MP4)
- PSP Videos (H.264 MP4)
- Cell Phone Videos (3GP)
- Flash Videos (FLV)
- MPEG Audio Layer 3 (MP3)

 - How to play a flash video in YouTuber?

1. Click Select Video.
2. Click Play to start playing.
3. If you want to play in fullscreen mode click Fullscreen.
4. To close fullscreen mode click Close.

 - I can't download videos from YouTube!

There are two possible reasons:

1. You are not connected to the internet.
2. The source of the website where the video you want to download is located has changed.

What can you do?

1.Check your internet connection.
2.Wait until an update is available for YouTuber.

 - Which websites are supported by YouTuber?

In this version the supported website is only YouTube.
